Who We Are

About Banana Mobile

Founded in 2009 and headquartered in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia, Banana Mobile Solutions is an established mobile-device lifecycle company integrating repair services, mobile spare-parts distribution, e-commerce, trade-in operations, refurbishment and device resale.

This demo business supports consumers, retailers and corporate partners through practical mobile-device solutions while promoting reuse and responsible recycling.
